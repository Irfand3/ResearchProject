Our Goal

Encouraging theoretical and applied researches in the field of operational research and developing the assosiation in the field of operational researches in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Promoting the international connections and gaining the prestige of the Bosnia and Herzegovina scientists

Our Team

Bosnia and Herzegovina Operations Research Assosiations Team is independent, voluntary, public organization which unites the experts in the field of operations research and their applications, actively working for the development of assosiation in the field of operations research in BiH
